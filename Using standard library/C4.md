## C4 ACME Global Widget Production

Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to design the ACME Global
Widget Production System.

1.  ACME has its **own production sites**, and uses **3rd party
    production sites**.
2.  All sites are connected to a **central production host**
3.  Each production site consists of several workstations
4.  Monitoring and Analytics is in the scope of what we are
    creating/changing - SupplyChain and InventoryTracking are not
